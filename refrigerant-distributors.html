Refrigerant Distributors

For critical components of air conditioning and refrigerant systems, our refrigerant distributors and brazed assemblies are the preferred choice of leading air conditioning and refrigeration OEMs, subsystem manufacturers, and national and regional distributors. As an added value, our engineering staff can work with you to develop innovative, economical design solutions for your specific needs. 

When we acquired KeepFlo in 2012, our customers were excited to see a familiar, trusted name join the line of Control Devices products that they have come to depend upon. Not only is a KeepFlo a leading manufacturer of refrigerant distributors, but they do it here in the United States.
